Our Story

Transforming the Dairy Landscape of Bihar through Science & Technology.

Our Mission

The Dairy Development Programme (DDP) Saharsa was founded with a single goal: to empower rural farmers by providing them with the same high-quality veterinary care and education available in urban centers.

From our Solar-Powered Farm to our GSM-controlled appliances, we integrate technology to make dairy farming profitable and sustainable.
